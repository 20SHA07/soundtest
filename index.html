GETTING STARTED

Welcome to the 16-Bit Music Loop Tool! Create your own retro-style music loops with this step sequencer.

CONTROLS

  • PLAY - Start playback of your loop
  • STOP - Stop playback
  • TEMPO - Adjust the speed (60-180 BPM)
  • SPACE BAR - Toggle play/stop

CREATING PATTERNS

Click on the grid buttons to activate/deactivate steps for each instrument:

  • KICK - Bass drum
  • SNARE - Snare drum
  • HI-HAT - Hi-hat cymbal
  • PERC - Additional percussion
  • BASS - Bass synthesizer
  • LEAD - Lead synthesizer
  • CHORD - Chord synthesizer
  • EFFECT - Special effects

VISUALIZATION

The waveform display shows the audio output in real-time, with a retro 16-bit style visualization.

TIPS

  • Start with a simple drum pattern (kick and snare)
  • Add hi-hats for rhythm
  • Layer in bass and lead sounds
  • Use effects sparingly for accent
